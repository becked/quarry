The Good

DIFFICULTYMODE_GOOD

Description

This is the baseline difficulty of the game. AI Nations start with some development, according to the game's premise that you are a new Nation in an old world of developed ones. Your economy is the same as the AI's, which is still inclined to be peaceful.
