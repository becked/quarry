Difficulty Levels

9 entries

The New
Suitable for a first experience. You will only end up at war if you want to. The AI is significantly weakened.
The Able
Suitable for an easy sandbox game. You will only end up at war if you want to, the AI is intentionally weakened.
The Just
Good choice for learning the game mechanics. You start in a better position than the AI, which will rarely attack.
The Good
This is the baseline difficulty of the game. AI Nations start with some development, according to the game's premise that you are a new Nation in an old world of developed ones. Your economy is the same as the AI's, which is still inclined to be peaceful.
The Strong
The first difficulty level where AI is not restricted in warfare. It will declare war more freely and will use Forced March. Tribal raids also become more common.
The Noble
Harder difficulties begin here, existing AI Nations are more developed and Cities near the map edges are no longer safe from Tribal raids.
The Glorious
The AI is more aggressive than on lower difficulties and can attack earlier. Tribal units get an additional fatigue point, making raiders considerably more dangerous than before.
The Magnificent
A very challenging difficulty where your economy is weaker and AI Nations begin with strong development. Avoiding early war is critical, AI Nations start with enough to easily crush you.
The Great
The highest difficulty, the AI gets a moderate economy bonus in addition to its starting development and will not wait long before it demands tribute or attacks outright. You will face many tribal raids and domestic rebels.
