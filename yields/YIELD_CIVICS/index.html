Help

Cities produce Specialists and Projects with their Civics. Unused Civics go into the Global stockpile, which can be used to enact Laws, establish Theologies, construct Wonders, Hurry Production, perform Missions like Pacify City, and other uses. You can stockpile a maximum of Civics, with excess converted into Science. Increase Civics by training Stonecutters and establishing Forums I. Members of your Court with high Charisma also contribute to the global Civics stockpile. In the case of a Deficit, one or more Cities will automatically switch Production to a Council.
