Discontent

YIELD_DISCONTENT

Help

When a City's Happiness bar empties, its overall Discontent Level goes up by one, which reduces Science and increases Maintenance. Higher Discontent Levels also reduce the Opinion of the City's ruling Family. Some ways to slow Discontent include connecting your City with the Trade Network, holding a Festival, adopting Laws such as Tolerance, keeping a City's Religions happy, and building Baths.
