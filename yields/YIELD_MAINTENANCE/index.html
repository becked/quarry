Maintenance

YIELD_MAINTENANCE

Help

Maintenance decreases the amount of Money a City generates. A City's Maintenance is increased by a higher Population, the City's distance from your Capital, and each Improvement within the City's borders. City Maintenance can be reduced by connecting the City to your Trade Network and improving the Opinion of the City's ruling Family.
