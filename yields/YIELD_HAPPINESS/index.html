Happiness

YIELD_HAPPINESS

Help

When a City's Happiness bar fills up, its overall Happiness Level goes up by one, which increases Growth and reduces Maintenance. Some ways to increase Happiness include connecting your City with the Trade Network, holding a Festival, adopting Laws such as Tolerance, keeping a City's Religions happy, and building Baths.
