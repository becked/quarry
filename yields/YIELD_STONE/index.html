Stone

YIELD_STONE

Help

The primary source of Stone is Quarries, which are most productive on Marble, on Arid Terrain, and adjacent to Mountains. Stone is needed primarily to build Improvements, such as Shrines, and Projects, such as Walls. In the case of a Deficit, Money will be spent to buy the missing resource.
