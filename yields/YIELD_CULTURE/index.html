Culture

YIELD_CULTURE

Help

When a City's Culture bar fills up, a positive Event occurs, and the City's overall Culture Level increases. The four Culture Levels are Weak, Developing, Strong, and Legendary. Culture Level affects which Improvements and Wonders can be built in a City's territory and whether the City can Hurry Production, which requires at least Developing Culture. Cities with higher Culture levels also cost additional Food, Iron, Stone, and Wood per Population. Ways to increase a City's Culture yield include building Shrines and Odeons, training certain Specialists, spreading a Religion to a City, and adopting certain Laws, such as Epics.
