Science

YIELD_SCIENCE

Help

Science increases the rate at which your Nation discovers new Technologies. Science yields can be increased by building Archives I and by training Specialists in your Cities. Laws such as Centralization and some religious Theologies can also help you generate more Science. Members of your Court with high Wisdom also contribute to Science yields.
