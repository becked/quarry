Food

YIELD_FOOD

Help

The best source of Food is usually Farms, which are most fruitful next to Fresh Water and on Lush Terrain. Food can also be acquired by building Nets and Pastures, and by training Farmers, Fishers, and Ranchers. Food is needed to feed your Population and to produce new Settlers. In the case of a Deficit, Money will be spent to buy the missing resource.
