Wood

YIELD_WOOD

Help

Wood can be gathered either by chopping down Trees or by building Lumbermills once Forestry has been researched. Wood is necessary for building some City Improvements, such as Ranges, Military Units, such as Archers, and Ship Units, such as Biremes. These units also require some Wood each turn as upkeep. In the case of a Deficit, Money will be spent to buy the missing resource.
