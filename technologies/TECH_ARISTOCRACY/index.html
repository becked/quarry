Aristocracy

TECH_ARISTOCRACY

Advice

Aristocracy is vital as it enables the appointment of an Ambassador, who can partake in diplomatic Missions to arrange a Peace or Truce, a Trade Mission, or even to Demand Tribute from another Nation. Aristocracy also unlocks the Centralization Law, which increases your production of Science and allows the improvement of Marsh Tiles, and the Vassalage Law, which reduces the burden of your army's upkeep if you are aiming to field a large force.
