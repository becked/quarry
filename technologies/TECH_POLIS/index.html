Polis

TECH_POLIS

Advice

Polis is useful for both defending and expanding your Cities. Building Walls increases the HP of a City and can increase Happiness if you keep a Unit from the same Family stationed in the walled City. Hamlets are Urban Improvements that can be built anywhere within your Territory. Over time they will develop into Villages and then Towns, consuming Food but providing increased yields of Money, especially when built on your Trade Network.
