Hydraulics

TECH_HYDRAULICS

Advice

Hydraulics support your Cities by allowing the construction of Watermills and Windmills, which provide a steady income of Science and greatly improve the output of adjacent Mines, Quarries, and Lumbermills. Hydraulics also unlocks the Ballista, an anti-Infantry Siege weapon. Although slow and short-ranged, the Ballista's deals Pierce damage that can hit two further enemies in a line behind the target at reduced damage.
