Vaulting

TECH_VAULTING

Advice

Vaulting can yield great benefits from World Religions in your Nation through the construction of Cathedrals for a bonus to Culture and Legitimacy, from the Iconography Law for bonuses from religious buildings, or from the Calligraphy Law for bonuses from Libraries and Specialists.
