Stirrups

TECH_STIRRUPS

Advice

Stirrups greatly enhance your mounted Unit capabilities through the recruitment of Horsemen, fast Units useful for flanking and finishing off units with their Rout ability, and Horse Archers, mounted ranged units who are able to quickly reposition or escape from a fight.
