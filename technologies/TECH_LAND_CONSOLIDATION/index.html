Land Consolidation

TECH_LAND_CONSOLIDATION

Advice

Land Consolidation makes use of the various resources within your City Territories. Expensive but powerful and versatile Camel Archers and War Elephants can be built in Cities where Camels and Elephants are respectively available. Groves can provide further Culture to develop your City and can be improved with a Gardener Specialist to gain one of the Luxuries used to gain favor with Families, Cities, or other Nations.
