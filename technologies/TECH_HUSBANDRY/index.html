Husbandry

TECH_HUSBANDRY

Advice

The humble-seeming Husbandry unlocks the building of Pastures on Tiles with grazing animals as a source of Growth and Food, or on Horses for Orders. Husbandry also leads to further Research that unlocks Mounted Units.
