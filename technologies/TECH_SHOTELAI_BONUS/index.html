Free Shotelai

TECH_SHOTELAI_BONUS

Advice

This bonus grants a free Shotelai Unit in your Capital. A melee Infantry Unit that is unique to Aksum, the Shotelai will Disarm its target, temporarily lowering their Strength for any future attacks. If a bonus card is not researched when it appears, it is permanently trashed once discarded and will not appear again.
