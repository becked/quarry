Portcullis

TECH_PORTCULLIS

Advice

Portcullis is a vital technology that unlocks the appointment of a Spymaster, who can be sent on Missions to undermine your enemies through Infiltration or Assassination. It also allows for the construction of Moats to further defend your Cities with existing Walls.
