Infantry Square

TECH_INFANTRY_SQUARE

Advice

The discovery of Infantry Square unlocks Pikemen, the height of Polearm infantry. Pikemen are devastating against mounted Units and attack with strong Pierce attacks, damaging the enemy behind the one they are attacking.
