Navigation

TECH_NAVIGATION

Advice

The first step in naval research, Navigation encourages exploration of the seas by allowing the automation of your Scouts and by unlocking Biremes. The Colonies Law can help you expand your Cities through buying surrounding Tiles, while Serfdom grants additional Orders to your Nation each turn.
