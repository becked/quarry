Sovereignty

TECH_SOVEREIGNTY

Advice

Sovereignty is an important step towards Siege Units and a useful tech for shaping your Nation. The laws of Tyranny and Constitution offer a choice between gaining resources by keeping military Units in your own Territory or by training more Specialists in your Cities.
