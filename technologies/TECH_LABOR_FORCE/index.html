Labor Force

TECH_LABOR_FORCE

Advice

Labor Force allows your Workers to add Roads to Tiles. Although Roads can be slow and expensive to build, they are essential in expanding your Trade Network and keeping your Cities Connected and can also provide a great way to quickly move your Units to the front-line. Labor Force also opens the choice between the Freedom and Slavery Laws, and it is the path towards researching both Polearm and naval Units.
