Metaphysics

TECH_METAPHYSICS

Advice

Metaphysics can help you control the Religions in your Nation through various Missions. With Metaphysics researched, your Leader will be able to convince other Characters to Convert to the State Religion to keep your court united by faith, while the High Synod Mission sends an Ambassador to try to improve relations with a Religion.
