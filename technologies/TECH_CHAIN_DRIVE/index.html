Chain Drive

TECH_CHAIN_DRIVE

Advice

Researching Chain Drive unlocks the Polybolos, one of the strongest Siege units and an upgrade to the Ballista. The Polybolos has a bonus against ranged Units and deals both Pierce and Cleave damage, making it useful for cutting through formations to hit the ranged Units behind.
