Monasticism

TECH_MONASTICISM

Advice

Monasticism encourages making good use of your Nation's Religion, as the Monotheism Law increases your Orders for each City that converts to your State Religion, while Polytheism allows the building of unlimited Pagan Shrines. The Monastery can be built by a Disciple of a World Religion to provide an income of Science.
