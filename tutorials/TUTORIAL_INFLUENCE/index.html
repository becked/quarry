Tutorial: Influence

TUTORIAL_INFLUENCE

Help

Influence Missions give you the chance to improve another Character's Opinion of your leader, at the cost of a few Orders and some Money. They may also lead to follow up Events.

You can conduct an Influence Mission by clicking the silhouette button in the action panel on the right while a Character other than your Leader is selected. You may influence members of any Nation, not just your own.
