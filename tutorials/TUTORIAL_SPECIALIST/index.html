Tutorial: Specialist

TUTORIAL_SPECIALIST

Help

A Specialist can be recruited from the City Screen by clicking the Button on an Improvement so long as you have an available Citizen. Although Improvements are already productive on their own, a Specialist can boost their yields.

Luxuries are special resources that you acquire by adding a Rural Specialist to an Improvement on the correct Tile. For example, you access Gems by adding a Miner Specialist to a Mine Improvement on a Gems Tile.

Urban Specialists, like Acolytes and Officers, can be trained to higher levels for further yield boosts.
