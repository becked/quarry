Tutorial: Undo

TUTORIAL_UNDO

Help

You can Undo as many decisions as you want, including moves, combat, building Improvements, and Event choices up to five past turns. Undo is performed with hotkey(HOTKEY_UNDO) or by pressing the backwards arrow button in the bottom left of the screen.
