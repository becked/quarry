Tutorial: Settler

TUTORIAL_SECOND_CITY

Help

Settlers are key to expanding your Nation because only they can found new Cities. Move a Settler to a City Site to be able to establish a new City.

City Sites can be claimed prior to sending a Settler to colonize them. Place a Unit of any type on the City Site Tile to claim it as your own, thereby preventing Foreign Settlers from founding a City there.

When you found a City, you will have to choose which Family to grant it to. This Family will provide powerful bonuses to the City. After granting a City to a third distinct Family, you will lose access to your nation's fourth Family, so carefully consider what bonuses you want and which Family you can go without.
