Tutorial: Tribes

TUTORIAL_TRIBES

Help

Once you've driven the local Tribes away from a City Site, you can claim it by placing a Unit on it. This will prevent the return of the Tribe and will also keep other Nations from founding a City there.

Tribes can engage in limited diplomacy but generally won't be coordinated in the way opposing Nations are. Finding a weak Tribe and claiming their City Sites for yourself will be an important step toward growing your Nation.

You may have to defeat some Barbarians to access more City Sites. Barbarians do not behave like Tribes as you are always at War with them. They are good targets for early expansion as they are easier to drive away than a more organized Tribe.
