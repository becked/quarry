Tutorial: Chancellor

TUTORIAL_CHANCELLOR

Help

You can use your Chancellor to help manage your realm with special Missions. Just select the portrait of your Chancellor in the top right, and you will see their available actions in the panel on below.

A Chancellor can give Family Gifts to improve relations with your powerful Families, lower Discontent with Pacify City, and even Imprison troublesome Characters!

If you have researched the Spoked Wheel technology and you have an available candidate on your court with the Zealot, Judge, or Builder trait, then you may appoint a Chancellor using the glowing quill pen icon in the top right of the screen.
