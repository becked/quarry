Tutorial: Intercession

TUTORIAL_INTERCESSION

Help

Intercession (Religion) and Intercession (Family) Missions let Pleased Family or Religion Heads in your nation speak to another Character on your behalf. At the cost of Orders and Money, Opinion: Character can be improved, and they can also lead to follow up events.

You can conduct these missions by selecting the character you wish to target (or, the Family or Religion Head) and then clicking the Intercession button in the top right Action Panel.

Religion Heads can intercede with almost anyone of that religion, even other leaders, while Family heads can intercede with members of that family.
