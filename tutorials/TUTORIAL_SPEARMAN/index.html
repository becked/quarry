Tutorial: Spearman

TUTORIAL_SPEARMAN

Help

The Spearman is the first Polearm Unit available to your Nation. Polearm Units excel against enemy Mounted Units. Only Polearm Units can exert a ZOC on Mounted Units. Spearmen also have a Pierce attack, striking the unit behind their target for partial damage.
