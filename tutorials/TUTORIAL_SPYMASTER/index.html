Tutorial: Spymaster

TUTORIAL_SPYMASTER

Help

You can use your Spymaster to conduct secret Missions! Just select the portrait of your Spymaster in the top right, and you will see their available actions in the panel below.

A Spymaster can execute missions like Infiltrate Nation, Steal Research, Assassinate, and more!

If you have researched the Portcullis technology and you have an available candidate on your court with the Tactician, Schemer, or Scholar trait, then you may appoint a Spymaster using the glowing eye icon in the top right of the screen.
