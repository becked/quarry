Tutorial: Ambassador

TUTORIAL_AMBASSADOR

Help

You can use your Ambassador to conduct diplomatic Missions. Just select the portrait of your Ambassador in the top right, and you will see their available actions in the panel below. An Ambassador can make Peace with Nations and Tribes, form a Trade Mission, offer and demand Tribute, and more!

Once you have researched the Aristocracy technology and you have an available candidate on your court with the Orator, Diplomat, or Commander Trait, then you may appoint an Ambassador using the glowing scroll icon in the top right of the screen.
