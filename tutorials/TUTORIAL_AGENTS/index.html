Tutorial: Agents

TUTORIAL_AGENTS

Help

With a Spymaster on your Council, you can convert Scouts into an Agent Network in a rival City (temporarily consuming the Scout in the process). If you move your Scout adjacent to the territory of that City, the Agent Network Button will be enabled in the Unit Panel on the left.

Once you have established an Agent Network in a City, an eligible Character can then be assigned as the local Agent there. To do so, you need to click the Agent Button below the City Banner.

A dedicated Agent Character can perform Insurrection and Treachery Missions and will siphon off a percentage of the City's Yields, depending on the Ratings of the Character. (For example, a Character's Wisdom will determine how much Science they will gather.)

You can choose Agent Missions to perform from the Agent Character's Panel, the Spymaster Character's Panel, or even directly from the City in question by clicking the Agent Button beneath the City banner.
