Tutorial: Ambitions

TUTORIAL_AMBITION

Help

Ambitions are goals that your Families would like for you to pursue, and completing them will grant you, and all of your descendants, a permanent boost to Legitimacy.

Completing an Ambition unlocks a new one and completing ten is one way to win via an Ambition Victory, so always do your best to fulfill your current Ambitions.
