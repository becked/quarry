Raiders

TRIBE_RAIDERS

Help

Raiders emerge from Tribal Sites which have too many Units. A random number of the Tribe's Units are turned into Raiders, who target a nearby City at War or Truce with the Tribe. However, Raiders are always at War with all Nations. (Tribes in a Tribal Alliance will never become Raiders.)
