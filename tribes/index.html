Tribes

10 entries

Rebels
Rebel Units are spawned from Cities with Families with negative Opinion. These Units will prefer to attack Units of the Nation from which they spawned. Rebel Units remember their original Family, so Units of the same Family receive a % combat modifier.
Anarchy
When a City is breached, it enters a state of Anarchy. This state continues until the City is fully captured by the Nation or Tribe that positions a military unit in its City center.
Raiders
Raiders emerge from Tribal Sites which have too many Units. A random number of the Tribe's Units are turned into Raiders, who target a nearby City at War or Truce with the Tribe. However, Raiders are always at War with all Nations. (Tribes in a Tribal Alliance will never become Raiders.)
Barbarians
Barbarians occupy City Sites like other Tribes, but they are in a permanent state of War with all Nations.
