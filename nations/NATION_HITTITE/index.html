Hatti

NATION_HITTITE

Story

I am Hattusili I, creator of nations and conqueror of empires. I bring fear to the hearts of Kings and Queens, and make Pharaohs kneel before me whether they admit it or not. In my mortal days I am a King and in immortality I am a God.

My people are the Hittites, the hidden people. Sense our presence until it is too late. Unwise you'd be to think we are not real or it will be much too late. Save your people and kneel before me.

I demand loyalty and the weak kneel before me. I denied my sons the throne, and with my passing my reign goes to my grandson. He will lead our people into eternity and keep my legacy.

I am Hattusili, King of Hattusa.
