Kush

NATION_KUSH

Story

I am Amanirenas, Qore and Kandake of the land of the Nine Bows. Even empires quake before my might.

As the Queen of Meroe, I ruled alone. I led my armies into Roman-occupied Egypt and returned with the bronze statue of their Emperor, then buried it beneath our temple steps to be trodden on by all. My husband and son fell, and our religious city was sacked, yet I went on the offensive and continued the fight until Rome agreed to peace.

Although fearsome in appearance with my one eye and pet lions, I also encouraged trade and peace with our neighbors. Under my rule, Kush was a powerful and independent nation, dedicated to Amani and Apedemek.

I am Amanirenas. Under my protection, Kush will never fall.
