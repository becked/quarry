Aksum

NATION_AKSUM

Story

I am Kaleb of Aksum, champion of faith.

In response to king Yūsuf Asʾar Yathʾar's persecution of Christians across the Red Sea, I invaded the land and defeated the despotic king, a feat that would one day earn me the title of Saint Elesbaan.

I went on to unite the Agaw tribes in my region, expanding the territory of my rule and securing important trade routes.

After many years of rule I abdicated the throne to pursue a quiet life of worship. What triumphs I achieve before then shall now be up to you.
