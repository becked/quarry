Babylonia

NATION_BABYLONIA

Story

I am Nebuchadnezzar, son of a Mesopotamian king, husband to Amytis of Medea, and forever a worshiper of the god Marduk. In the name of my father, I elevated my city Babylon to be the jewel of the region. I have trained in military tactics, conquered kingdoms, and crushed rebellions.

I built The Hanging Gardens for my wife so she could feel closer to the hills of Persia and The Ishtar Gate to honor Marduk. I connected them both with the Processional Way, and all visitors are bewildered by the beauty of my empire.

Under my rule, women have equal rights to men. We create intellectual institutes and temples, and my focus is on art, culture, science, mathematics, and craftsmanship. In a decade, I turned Babylon into a city of light.
