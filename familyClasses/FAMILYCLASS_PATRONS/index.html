Patrons

FAMILYCLASS_PATRONS

Advice Found

The Cities of Patrons produce extra Civics, which allows them to train Specialists more quickly. Further, each Precious Resource increases the City's Culture yield. The Patron Family Seat also grants a free Court Minister. Each Patron City starts with a Literature Luxury, which can be used to boost Culture or gain favor.
