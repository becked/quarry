Statesmen

FAMILYCLASS_STATESMEN

Advice Found

Statesmen Cities boost your Orders and Civics yields. Their Cities produce more Civics as the Family's Opinion of you improves. As a result of these bonuses to Civics yields, Statesmen Cities are effective at training Specialists, which also cost them less Food.
