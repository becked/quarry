Artisans

FAMILYCLASS_ARTISANS

Advice Found

Artisans generate extra Culture and receive a significant bonus to Mines and Lumbermills. They are a good choice for areas with many Hills and Trees. The Artisan Family Seat is granted a free Worker, and Urban Improvements for that City are built more quickly.
