Traders

Advice Found

In Cities belonging to Traders, each Specialist produces Money. The Trader Family Seat is able to produce Caravans and also grants you with a Court Merchant as well as a Fair, both of which boost Money considerably. Trader Workers can build several Roads per turn, which makes them especially good at extending your Trade Network.
