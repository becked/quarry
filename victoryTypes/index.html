Victory Types

6 entries

Points
Victory from acquiring the target number of Victory Points, determined by the number of City Sites on the map and the number of Players or Teams competing.
Double
Victory from acquiring twice as many Victory Points as any other Nation.
(Must also have at least half the target Victory Points. Only possible for Human Players.)
Ambition
Victory from completing Ambitions, including one National Ambition.
(Only possible for Human Players.)
Time
Victory from having the most Victory Points at the end of 200 turns. Disabling this victory removes the turn limit.
Conquest
Victory from conquering all other Nations.
Alliance
Joint victory by having a National Alliance with the winner.
