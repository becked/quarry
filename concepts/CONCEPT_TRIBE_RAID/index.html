Tribal Raid

CONCEPT_TRIBE_RAID

Help

Raids emerge from Tribal Sites which have too many Units. A random number of the Tribe's Units are turned into Raiders, who target a nearby City at War or Truce with the Tribe. However, Raiders are always at War with all Nations. Tribes in a Tribal Alliance will never initiate Raids. At higher difficulties, Raids can emerge from distant lands at the border of the map.
