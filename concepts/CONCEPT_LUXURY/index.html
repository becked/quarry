Luxuries

CONCEPT_LUXURY

Help

Luxuries can be traded to a City increase Happiness. They can also be traded to other Nations, Tribes, and Families to improve their Opinion of you.

Family Classes all have two specific Luxuries which give them a higher Opinion boost. (For example, Clerics prefer Incense and Lavender.)

Luxuries come from the following Resources:
Salt
Gems
Fur
Honey
Incense
Lavender
Olives
Wine
Dyes
Pearls
