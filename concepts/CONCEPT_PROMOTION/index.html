Promotion

CONCEPT_PROMOTION

Help

Promotions provide various combat bonuses to Units. They are earned when leveling up from either acquiring XP or spending some Training. Each time a Unit chooses a Promotion, three new ones are randomly chosen from the eligible pool for the Unit's next Level. (Units have a maximum of Levels.)

Units also can temporarily inherit Promotions from the Traits of their Generals.
