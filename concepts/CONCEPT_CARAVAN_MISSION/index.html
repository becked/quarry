Caravan Mission

CONCEPT_CARAVAN_MISSION

Help

Caravan Units can be sent on a Caravan Mission to a Foreign Capital. The Caravan moves once per Turn (costing one Order), and upon safe arrival, the Unit is consumed, producing a lump sum of Money and an Opinion: Foreign boost.
