Trade Outpost

CONCEPT_TRADE_OUTPOST

Help

Trade Outposts generate Money and grant a small Tribe Opinion bonus for turns before consuming the City Site and turning into a City owned by the player. Establishing a Trade Outpost consumes the unit and can only be established with a non-hostile Tribal City Site. The cost of establishing a Trade Outpost scales with Tribe Opinion. If War is declared between the Tribe and the owner of the Trade Outpost, progress towards a City will be paused for the duration of the war.
